Compliance

Our commitment to regulatory standards

PMT Markets is committed to operating with the highest standards of regulatory compliance and ethical business practices. This Compliance page outlines our approach to meeting legal and regulatory requirements in the prediction market industry.

1. Regulatory Framework

PMT Markets operates in accordance with applicable financial regulations in jurisdictions where we provide services. We monitor regulatory developments and adapt our operations to maintain compliance with:

  • Financial services regulations relevant to prediction markets
  • Consumer protection laws
  • Data protection and privacy regulations
  • Anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing laws
  • Market conduct and fair trading standards

2. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Program

We maintain a comprehensive AML program designed to prevent our Platform from being used for money laundering or terrorist financing activities:

  • Customer Due Diligence: Enhanced verification for high-risk customers
  • Transaction Monitoring: Automated systems to detect suspicious activity
  • Reporting: Timely filing of suspicious activity reports to relevant authorities
  • Record Keeping: Maintenance of AML records for required periods
  • Training: Regular AML training for relevant employees

3. Know Your Customer (KYC) Procedures

We implement robust KYC procedures to verify the identity of our users and assess their risk profiles:

  • Identity verification using government-issued documents
  • Address verification through utility bills or bank statements
  • Source of funds verification for significant transactions
  • Ongoing monitoring of customer activity and risk profiles
  • Enhanced due diligence for politically exposed persons (PEPs)

4. Market Integrity & Fair Trading

We are committed to maintaining market integrity and preventing abusive trading practices:

  • Prohibition of market manipulation and insider trading
  • Monitoring for wash trading and other abusive patterns
  • Transparent pricing and settlement mechanisms
  • Clear disclosure of market rules and trading conditions
  • Independent verification of event outcomes

5. Risk Management

Our risk management framework includes:

  • Credit risk assessment for counterparty exposures
  • Operational risk controls for system reliability
  • Liquidity risk management for settlement obligations
  • Legal and regulatory risk monitoring
  • Business continuity and disaster recovery planning

6. Compliance Training & Culture

We foster a culture of compliance through:

  • Regular compliance training for all employees
  • Clear reporting lines for compliance issues
  • Whistleblower protection for reporting violations
  • Independent compliance monitoring and testing
  • Board-level oversight of compliance matters

7. Regulatory Reporting & Cooperation

We maintain open communication with regulatory authorities and fulfill our reporting obligations, including:

  • Regular regulatory filings and disclosures
  • Cooperation with regulatory examinations and inquiries
  • Timely notification of material events or changes
  • Maintenance of required licenses and registrations

8. Contact Compliance

For compliance inquiries or to report potential violations, please contact: compliance@pmtmarkets.com

Note: This Compliance page provides general information about our compliance program. It does not constitute legal advice. Users should consult with their own legal advisors regarding compliance matters.
